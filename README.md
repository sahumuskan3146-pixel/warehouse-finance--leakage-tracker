# Warehouse Finance Leakage Tracker

## 📊 Project Overview
A specialized operational finance tracker built to isolate invisible financial capital drain, overhead inefficiencies, and supply chain inventory mismatches. Focused on strict bottom-line protection, this engine identifies precisely where operational costs are eating into net profit margins.

## 🛠️ Core Tech Stack & Financial Analyst Skills
*   **Python (Operational Ingestion):** Developed programmatic pipelines to compare physical log datasets directly against financial invoice records to isolate variances.
*   **SQL (Variance Discovery):** Formulated highly specific lookup queries to identify pricing anomalies, duplicate supplier invoices, and temporal processing lag costs.
*   **Financial Modeling (Cost-Benefit & Variance Analysis):** Modeled baseline warehouse processing costs against live overhead streams to pinpoint variance discrepancies.

## 📈 Key Features
*   **Automated Leakage Diagnostics:** Instantly identifies transaction gaps where physical inventory velocity does not match recorded financial billing streams.
*   **Overhead Expense Analyzer:** Breaks down warehouse cost centers programmatically, highlighting anomalies in utility consumption or labor over-allocations.
*   **Supplier Invoice Auditing Module:** Automatically checks incoming supplier billing data against purchase order matrices to stop overcharges at the gate.

## 💡 Financial Impact & ROI
*   **Direct Bottom-Line Reclamation:** Plugs operational leaks that directly reduce EBITDA margins, converting hidden waste back into corporate cash reserves.
*   **Audit Readiness:** Creates a pristine, continuous electronic ledger trail of inventory capital value, dramatically lowering year-end internal audit friction.
